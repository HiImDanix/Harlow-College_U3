Spreadsheet development Unit 9: Spreadsheet development

In this unit we were learning about spreadsheets. Our scenario was that we were completing a work placement with the local football club in its administration department and had been asked to produce a spreadsheet to manage information about the club’s season-ticket holders.

For assignment A, we had to show our understanding of spreadsheets. We talked about the tools/technique usage, the purpose of it, strengths/weaknesses how features can improve it etc.

For assignment B, we had to design the spreadsheet for the local football club (structure, formatting etc

For assignment C, we had to develop the spreadsheet with a given realistic data set. Then we had to test the spreadsheet, gather feedback and refine/improve it.

For assignment D, we had to review the finished spreadsheet, evaluate and justify it.
