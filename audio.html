Audio Unit 5: Creating Digital Audio

In this unit we had to learn about digital audio and how to edit it.

For assignment A, we had to investigate two different audio products. Our investigation had to result in an evaluation of the strengths/weaknesses of the audio products, the features of it, the purpose etc. etc.

For assignment B, we had to plan our own two audio products. One of them had to be a comedy show that we were doing as a group work and the other one a scientific discovry that we did seperately. We made the script, planned our timeline, the effects that we were going to use, the assets etc.

For assignment C, we had to develop the audio products.You can see the final result at the bottom of the table, you will find there a play button that you can click on.

For assignment D, we took feedback from others, refined/improved it, reviewed/evaluated it etc.
